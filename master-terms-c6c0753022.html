1. Definitions

  • "Content" means posts, captions, profile copy, and other material created for the Client's LinkedIn presence.
  • "Deliverables" means Content and reports produced under an Order Form.

  • "Order Form" means a signed document setting out scope, fees, and term, incorporating this Agreement.

2. Services

Gravity Linked provides brand and communications advisory services, which may include LinkedIn strategy and content management, executive personal branding, workshops, book-writing guidance, and related advisory services, as set out in the applicable Order Form. Content writing, scheduling, and posting under this Agreement are provided exclusively for LinkedIn; Gravity Linked does not write or post content on any other social media platform or digital channel unless expressly agreed in a separate Order Form. The specific scope, deliverable volumes, posting frequency, and reporting cadence for each engagement are set out in the applicable Order Form. Gravity Linked may adjust its internal processes and AI Tools at any time without notice, provided this does not materially reduce the agreed Deliverables. Gravity Linked may engage subcontractors and third-party service providers (including third-party artificial intelligence models and platforms) to perform any part of the Services, provided Gravity Linked remains responsible for the performance of the Services.

3. Account Access & Client Obligations

Where required, the Client shall grant Gravity Linked the administrative permissions necessary to operate the Client's LinkedIn account solely for the purpose of providing the Services. The Client remains the account owner and is responsible for compliance with LinkedIn Terms. The Client shall: (a) provide accurate and complete Client Materials in a timely manner; (b) ensure Client Materials do not infringe any third-party rights and that all factual statements contained in Client Materials are accurate; (c) notify Gravity Linked promptly of material changes to its business or branding; (d) designate a named contact responsible for approvals; and (e) comply with LinkedIn Terms at all times. The Client is the publisher of record for all Content posted to its LinkedIn account and bears ultimate responsibility for published Content.

7. Term and Termination

This Agreement continues until all Order Forms have expired or been terminated. Order Forms auto-renew for successive equal periods unless either party provides thirty (30) days' written notice of non-renewal. Either party may terminate immediately for material uncured breach (after fourteen (14) days' written notice) or insolvency. On termination: all rights under the Order Form cease; the Client revokes Gravity Linked's LinkedIn access; each party returns or destroys the other's Confidential Information; and accrued payment obligations survive.
